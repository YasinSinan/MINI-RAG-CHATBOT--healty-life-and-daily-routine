Exercise Tips
Benefits of Exercise
Exercise improves cardiovascular health and strengthens muscles.
It also reduces anxiety and improves mood by releasing endorphins.
Recommended Frequency
Experts recommend exercising at least 3–5 times per week.
Types of Exercise
	•	Cardio (walking, running, cycling)
	•	Strength training
	•	Flexibility exercises (yoga, stretching)
