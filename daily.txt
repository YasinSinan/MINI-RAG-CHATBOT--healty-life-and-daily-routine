daily_routine.txtDaily Routine GuideA productive daily routine can improve mental clarity and reduce stress.Morning:* Wake up at the same time every day.* Drink a glass of water.* Do light stretching or short exercise.Afternoon:* Take short breaks while working.* Avoid heavy meals to prevent fatigue.Evening:* Avoid screens at least one hour before sleep.* Read a book or practice relaxation techniques.Consistency is key to maintaining a good routine.
